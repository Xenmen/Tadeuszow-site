Sporadically, Daniel does things involving music or general audio. I have mixtapes @ Mixcloud, and songs I've made @ Bandcamp page, as Xenmen. I'm presently more interested in recreating old electronic music than original works, and remixing the entire Perfect Dark N64 soundtrack is a life goal (presently, 4 tracks have been midimapped and are in varying stages of arrangement).

Ableton is the DAW of choice.

If you use Linux as your primary workstation, I have good news for you! There are plenty more options than there used to be. I find Bitwig to be an exceptionally well engineered tool, though it is not freedom respecting. Take a look at this list for some options.

If you are in need of acoustic foam, I would recommend Foam By Mail.
