Minetest Raidcraft modpack port under consideration

Minetest is an LGPL alternative to Minecraft written in C++, which already has a solid mod api and mod downloader. This makes Minetest a very attractive target for porting Kingsquee's Raidcraft mod pack, since Raidcraft can no longer be updated for Minecraft. Raidcraft is an experience distinct from other modpacks as it forces the player to act boldly early on, and pressures the player to raid difficult dungeons just to have sufficient supplies to survive. It is a fantastical hunter-gatherer simulation, which also takes into account invasion by Mongol hordes. Suffice to say, it's a challenge on par with Dwarf Fortress, and worthy of being preserved in a new medium.

The full viability of Minetest for a Raidcraft port will be determined sometime around July of 2015, and if viable a preliminary build will be released about that time.


Researching Technology for Liberty

My foremost goal in life is to reduce recurring monetary costs as much as possible, so that I can spend more of my life writing, reading, and researching with people that I get along with. I've identified three areas of focus: Housing Costs, Food Costs, and Information Costs. To seriously advance on these fronts requires sorting through an enormous amount of information, and even the amount I've already gathered is not yet well organized. If you have any research you feel would be useful to these projects, or suggestions that you think may help, please consider sending Daniel an email, a message on IRC, or modifying the website yourself and making a 'pull request' on github.
